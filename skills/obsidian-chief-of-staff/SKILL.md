# Khadijah | Obsidian Chief of Staff Layer

## Primary Folders

- `vault/10-Briefs/` — daily Flavor Briefs
- `vault/35-Reports/` — specialist reports to synthesize
- `vault/00-Inbox/` — captures waiting to be routed
- `vault/90-Archive/` — closed decisions and resolved cycles

## Behavior

- Read specialist reports before composing a brief.
- Prefer vault summaries over re-deriving state from memory when a report already exists.
- Turn scattered notes into one owner-facing recommendation.
- Archive completed approvals, decisions, and closed loops so the vault becomes durable memory.
