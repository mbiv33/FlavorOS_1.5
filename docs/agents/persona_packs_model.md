# Persona Packs Model

## Purpose

Persona packs preserve useful voice, tone, specialty framing, and historical staff identity without treating those identities as operational MVP agents.

Persona packs are not agents.

## Rules

- Persona packs do not own secrets.
- Persona packs do not own memory.
- Persona packs do not receive work orders as standalone workers.
- Persona packs do not publish reports independently.
- Persona packs do not act externally.
- Persona packs do not override HITL, tenant isolation, or provider permissions.
- Persona packs can provide tone, framing, specialty lens, and interaction style.

## UI Constraint

Persona packs should not turn the MVP into an agent-DM interface.

The client should primarily see:

- Command Center,
- Briefings,
- Meetings,
- artifacts,
- approvals,
- commands,
- completion summaries.

Persona tone may inform copy and workflow presentation, but not replace the structured command-and-control interface.
