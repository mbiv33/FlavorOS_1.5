# Persona Packs

## Purpose

FlavorOS preserves retired staff identities as persona packs. Persona packs add presence, story, tonal variety, and latency cover during briefings or longer voice interactions.

Persona packs are not agents.

## Rules

- Persona packs do not own secrets.
- Persona packs do not receive NATS work orders.
- Persona packs do not publish reports.
- Persona packs do not act externally.
- Persona packs do not override the active agent's authority boundary.
- Persona packs can provide anecdotes, analogies, emotional texture, and "remote worker" flavor.

## Invocation Pattern

Human-facing agents may invoke persona material when:

- filling natural pause time during voice calls,
- transitioning between briefing sections,
- making a dry operational update easier to absorb,
- giving relationship/social framing,
- surfacing wellness and PERMA-V context through Sinclair,
- explaining logistics/ops implications in plain language.

Example:

```text
Khadijah answers the real question.
Sinclair notes the follow-up.
Khadijah uses a short Regine-style aside to make the social implication memorable.
The system continues processing the deeper tool call in the background.
```
